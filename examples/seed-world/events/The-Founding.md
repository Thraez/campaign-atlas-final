When the first pine cutters laid down their axes and the wardens of the
wood laid down their bows, they swore the treaty of the moot-stone and
agreed to share the forest's bounty.

This event marks year **0** of the *After Establishment* (AE) calendar.
The settlement that grew up around the stone is now called [[Pinemoot]].
