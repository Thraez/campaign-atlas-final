The Watch is older than any town it now serves. Its members wear grey
cloaks and a pine-sprig pin, and they answer only to their captains.

In Pinemoot the Watch keeps the gate, walks the wall, and escorts traders
to the next post down the road. Elsewhere they ride alone, and elsewhere
again they have not been seen for a generation.
