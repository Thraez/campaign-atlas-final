Pinemoot grew up around a moot-stone where the pine cutters first swore
their treaty with the Watch. The town keeps fewer than a thousand souls,
most of them woodcutters, charcoal burners, or trappers working the edge
of the great forest.

The garrison is small but professional, led by [[Captain Ren]]. Mist clings
to the streets most mornings until the cookfires drive it back, and the
bell on the moot-stone is rung at dusk to call wanderers home before the
wood closes in.
