# Thornhold

Thornhold is a fortified mining city perched above the [[Raven's Vale]].
It trades iron and rare resonance crystal with [[Sunhaven]] along the King's Road.

%% DM NOTE: The lord-mayor secretly funds expeditions into the [[Deeproot Cavern]]. %%

## History

Founded after the Sundering, Thornhold's walls were raised from the same red
stone that gives the city its name. Travelers know it by its alias *The Red City*.

- The Iron Gate
