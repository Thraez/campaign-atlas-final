# Deeproot Cavern (DM)

%% This entire entry is DM canon. %%

Beneath [[Raven's Vale]] lies a network of crystalline tunnels. The lord-mayor
of [[Thornhold]] has been quietly extracting resonance crystal here for two
decades. The deeper galleries are rumored to house something older.
