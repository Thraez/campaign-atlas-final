When the Twin Moons collided in the night sky, the firmament tore and the seas
boiled. Half of the old continent slid beneath the waves; what remained
became the Astrath Deeprealm. Survivors founded the great cities of
[[Thornhold]] and [[Sunhaven]] in the years that followed.

This event marks year **0** of the *After Sundering* calendar.
