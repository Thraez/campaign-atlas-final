# Edric
*Harwick's old companion, riding hard for Stavall.*

Edric was the [[Vein-Marked]] companion of [[Harwick]], the previous owner of [[Ashbrook Manor]]. After the Ashbrook sequence, he rode ahead of the party toward [[Stavall]], setting a hard pace that suggests he knows where he is going.


![[Edric.png]]
## Appearance
Edric is lean and windburned, with sandy hair tied back, tired sharp eyes, and faint blue vein-marks running up one side of his neck.

%%
## DM Notes

### Truth

Edric is a chase target, not yet a confrontation. The party is racing him to Stavall. He may be carrying intelligence the facility wants, fleeing toward the only people who would shelter him, or both. He may be heading for the same facility as Mars.

The [[Wakewall Gate]]'s civilian clerk's ledger gives the party the chance to confirm whether he reached the city, when, and under what name. [[Thresh]] reads the gate ledger by evening; he may notice Edric before the party does.

### Plot hooks

- The party can catch him on the road, intercept him at the gate, or arrive too late and find him already inside the facility.
- His relationship to Harwick is the throughline. Was he Harwick's protector, his betrayer, or his fellow believer?
